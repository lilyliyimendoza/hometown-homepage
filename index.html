Dajia, Taiwan

Welcome to Dajia, a blend of natural wonders and cultural treasures, offering a rich tapestry of history, folklore, and warm hospitality alongside specialty farm products, historic sites, and locally-produced crafts for an unforgettable journey of discovery.

Top three activities to do at Dajia

Natural Wonders

Discover the beauty of the Dajia River, Da'an River, and Tiezhen Mountain, perfect for hiking, picnicking, or simply soaking in the serene surroundings.

Dajia Mazu Pilgrimage

Join over a million pilgrims in March at Jenn Lann Temple for one of the world's top religious celebrations.

Local Delights

Delight in Dajia's specialty farm products like award-winning taro desserts at the vibrant night market.
